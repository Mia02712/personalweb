Self introduction

I am currently a senior student pursuing my Bachelor's degree. I have spent three years studying at Wenzheng College of Soochow University, majoring in Internet of Things Engineering. Additionally, I had the privilege of studying for one year at the University of Idaho, majoring in Computer Science.

In my three years at Wenzheng College, I delved into a wide range of subjects in the field of Internet of Things Engineering. I studied key concepts such as sensor technology, embedded systems, and communication protocols, which form the foundational knowledge required for building smart devices and IoT applications. I also actively participated in projects related to smart agriculture, smart cities, and smart homes, which honed my problem-solving and teamwork skills. Through active engagement in academic research and practical projects, I achieved excellent academic performance in the field of Internet of Things Engineering.

During my year at the University of Idaho, I immersed myself in the core areas of computer science. I engaged in coursework that covered various aspects, including data structures, algorithm design, database management, artificial intelligence, and more. This year of study provided me with an opportunity to explore the depths of computer science, enriching my programming skills and problem-solving abilities.

My dual-degree background equips me with both the technical prowess of Internet of Things Engineering and the software skills of Computer Science. I look forward to merging these two knowledge domains to build a strong foundation for my future career.

Research Experience

Project Name: Intelligent Agriculture (July 2023)

Research Aim: The objective of this project was to leverage IoT technology to enhance the efficiency and quality of agricultural production while concurrently reducing production costs and resource consumption

Research Process: To achieve this aim, I installed temperature and humidity sensor equipment within the agricultural production environment. I used hardware equipment and software tools to monitor environmental data in real-time, ensuring that the conditions were optimal for crop growth. Subsequently, I conducted comprehensive data analysis and processing to provide valuable data and technical support for intelligent agricultural management. This research not only deepened my understanding of IoT technology but also offered innovative solutions for sustainable agriculture and food production.

Through this research experience, I learned how to apply technology to address real-world challenges, handle vast amounts of data, and collaborate with teams to achieve common goals. I look forward to applying these experiences to future research projects and continuing to contribute to the advancement of science and technology.
